FADE IN:

EXT. FOREST - DAY

GIRL holding a teddy bear and MOTHER visits a tombstone in the middle of the day. MOTHER leaves GIRL in front of the tombstone alone.

GIRL is tearfully paying respects (in prayer) to the tombstone. GHOST somewhat resembling a human appears from being a tree settled on the tombstone. The GHOST is unnoticed by GIRL initially.

GHOST sits on top of the tombstone and consoles GIRL by putting his ghostly hand on GIRLâ€™s shoulder. GIRL looks up and notices GHOST.

GIRL stares at GHOST for a moment in shock but eventually seems to recognize GHOST. Both gaze at each other. GIRL rushes to hug with a tearful expression GHOST. GHOST reciprocates.

MOTHER calls back from a distance to signal GIRL it is time to go. GIRL hesitates and stays in place hugging GHOST. GHOST lets go and gestures to the girl but refuses to leave. Eventually GHOST (somehow) convinces GIRL to leave. GIRL eventually accepts but offers a teddy bear to GHOST, which GHOST accepts.

As GIRL walks away towards MOTHER, GIRL turns back to look at GHOST sitting on a tombstone. The GHOST vanishes, and the teddy bear is settled on top of the tombstone. GIRL and MOTHER leave.

Last shot is an image on the tombstone, with a teddy bear sitting on top of the tombstone.

END.

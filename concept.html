ENVIRONMENT

The forest the girl awakens in. It’s a strange forest that feels familiar and yet unrecognizable to her. The forest appears to be dream-like and whimsical. The forest feels warm and friendly in areas near the giant, but dark and dangerous in areas where the giant is not.

GENTLE FOREST GIANT

A friendly, dweller of the forest. Often followed around by the little creatures of the forest. Is curious about the young girl’s arrival in the forest, and wants to protect her. Somehow, the forest seems safer when he’s around.

LITTLE GIRL

The main protagonist. She is innocent and curious. She awakens in the forest with no memory of how she got there.
